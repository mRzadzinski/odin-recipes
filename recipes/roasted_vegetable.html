Roasted Vegetable Paste

Description

Time 15 min + 40 min (roasting and cooling)
Servings: 14 — 100 kcal

Inspiration: agamasmaka.pl

Ingredients

  • baking paper
  • 8 cloves garlic
  • 2 large onions
  • 2 large bell peppers
  • 12 sun-dried tomatoes in oil
  • 6 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp parsley
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps

  1. baking paper
    • Line a baking tin with paper
    • Set the oven to 340°F / 170°C (upper and lower heat)
  2. garlic
    • Peel
    • Spread on the baking tin
  3. onions
    • Peel
    • Cut in quarters
    • Spread on the baking tin
  4. bell peppers
    • Peel
    • Cut in quarters
    • Remove seeds
    • Spread on the baking tin
  5. -
    • Put in pre-heated oven
    • Roast
    • Let them cool
    • Peel the pepper
    • Place the vegetables in a bowl
  6. sun-dried tomatoes
    • Drain the oil
    • Place in a bowl
  7. olive oil, parsley, basil, salt
    • Add
    • Blend using hand blender
