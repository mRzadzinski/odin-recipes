Potato & Cucumber Salad

Time 30 min

Inspiration: olgasmile.com

Ingredients

  • 4 onions
  • 2 tbs oil
  • 7 potatoes
  • 5 cucumbers (in brine or pickled)
  • 5 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/5 tsp turmeric
  • himalayan pink salt

Steps

  1. onions
    • Peel
    • Dice
  2. oil + 1/4 tsp himalayan pink salt
    • Heat up oil in a pan
    • Add onion and salt
    • Stew
    • Remove the lid
    • Fry
    • Place in a bowl
  3. potatoes + 1 tsp salt + 1/5 tsp turmeric
    • Peel
    • Wash
    • Dice
    • Boil
    • Drain
    • Place in the bowl
  4. cucumbers
    • Dice finely
    • Place in the bowl
  5. mayonnaise + black pepper
    • Add
    • Mix
