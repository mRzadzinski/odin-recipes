Cream of Leek

Description

Time 35 min + 30 min (boiling)
Servings: 3 â€” 240 kcal

Inspiration: zakochanewzupach.pl

Ingredients

  • 1 leek
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • 4 potatoes
  • 1 level tsp salt
  • 2/3 tsp nutmeg (powder)
  • 1/3 tsp white pepper

Steps

  1. leek
    • Cut off green part
    • Remove the outer layer
    • Wash
    • Chop
  2. onion + garlic
    • Peel
    • Chop
  3. oil
    • Heat up in a pot
    • Add leek, onion and garlic
    • Stew (covered)
    • Remove the lid
    • Fry (stir often)
  4. potatoes
    • Peel
    • Chop
    • Add to the pot
  5. salt, nutmeg and white pepper
    • Add to the pot
  6. pot
    • Add water
    • Bring to a boil
    • Boil
    • Blend
